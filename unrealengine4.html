Unreal Engine 4

Using UnrealEngine 4 and VisualStudio 2017, I made a simple third person shooter that can be played in singleplayer or multiplayer mode. I think videos of the various stages of creating game will show better than a description the result of my work in the various stages of game development. I created the game myself. I spent over a year on it.

Result

This game is a third person shooter. The goal is to survive waves of incoming enemy bots and exploding balls. You can play with friends. Multiplayer network connection is based on the AdvancedSteamSession plugin. I was inspired by Hi-Life and Counter-Strike.

During the development of this game, I had the opportunity to better understand C++, UnrealEngine 4 and blueprints. I could implement a lot interesting solutions.
